About Us

Your trusted partner for international travel from the UAE

Our Story

IND ARAB TOURS AND TRAVELS was founded with a clear purpose: to bring world-class travel experiences within easy reach of UAE residents. Based in Sharjah, our agency was born out of a passion for exploration and a deep understanding of what today's discerning travelers truly need — authentic, well-organized journeys that go beyond the ordinary.

Our founding team brings together extensive travel industry expertise, with a shared commitment to crafting itineraries that balance discovery, comfort, and value. Every destination we offer has been carefully evaluated to ensure it delivers a genuinely memorable experience, whether you're seeking cultural immersion, adventure, relaxation, or a mix of everything.

As a UAE-based travel agency, we understand the unique preferences and requirements of residents traveling internationally — from visa facilitation to seasonal planning and cultural considerations. We are here to make your global travel aspirations a reality, one thoughtfully planned trip at a time.

Our Mission

To make international travel accessible, seamless, and memorable for every traveler departing from the UAE, with personalized itineraries and trusted expertise.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted travel agency for UAE-based travelers seeking authentic, well-organized global experiences.
