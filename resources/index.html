Turkey Travel Guide

Europe / Middle East Apr – Jun, Sep – Nov

Turkey bridges two continents and offers an incredible variety of experiences. Istanbul's historic Sultanahmet district, the otherworldly landscapes of Cappadocia, and the mineral terraces of Pamukkale are just the beginning. UAE residents enjoy easy visa access, making Turkey one of the most convenient international destinations. The Turkish Riviera along the Mediterranean coast provides stunning beach resorts and ancient ruins side by side.

Georgia Travel Guide

Caucasus / Europe Apr – Jun, Sep – Oct

Georgia is rapidly becoming a favorite destination for UAE travelers, and for good reason. Visa-free entry, affordable luxury, and a cuisine that rivals the world's best make it an easy choice. Explore Tbilisi's sulfur baths and modern art scene, venture into the Caucasus Mountains for hiking and ancient tower villages, or tour the Kakheti wine region — one of the oldest winemaking regions on Earth. The warmth of Georgian hospitality is legendary.

Enquire About Georgia

Egypt Travel Guide

North Africa / Middle East Oct – Apr

Egypt needs little introduction — its ancient civilization is one of humanity's greatest achievements. From the Pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx to the Valley of the Kings in Luxor, every corner tells a story thousands of years old. A Nile River cruise between Luxor and Aswan is a must-do experience. For UAE travelers, Egypt offers excellent value, straightforward visa arrangements, and direct flights from major UAE airports. Don't miss the Red Sea coast for world-class snorkeling and diving.

Travel Checklists

Documentation Checklist

  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
  • Visa for your destination (check requirements well in advance)
  • Flight tickets (printed and digital copies)
  • Travel insurance policy documents
  • Hotel booking confirmations
  • Travel itinerary with contact numbers
  • International driving permit (if planning to drive)
  • Emergency contact information and embassy details
  • Copies of all important documents stored in cloud/phone

Smart Packing Checklist

  • Universal power adapter (UAE uses Type G/C/D plugs)
  • Weather-appropriate clothing and layers
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Basic first-aid kit and personal medications
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Travel-size toiletries
  • Portable charger and USB cables
  • Neck pillow for long-haul flights
  • Lightweight daypack for excursions
